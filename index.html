Projects

Pusheen Travel

Pusheen Travel is my capstone project from the Grace Hopper Program. Pusheen Travel is a mobile app that gives users the ability to plan and keep track of their trips in a fun game-like way! Users are able to create maps with markers representing the places they'd like to visit on their trip. As the user travels, they can "collect" Pusheen images as they reach each destination on their map.

CookingBuddy

For my 3-day hackathon project at Grace Hopper, I built CookingBuddy, a virtual recipe book to make online recipes more hands-free. Users can upload a recipe they like, then walk through each step of the recipe on a separate page complete with a timer and how-to videos for any techniques that may be unfamiliar.

The Bling Ring

The Bling Ring is a full stack e-commerce site built from scratch. It includes products filterable by category, working login, signup, and OAuth capabilities as well as a working cart model built in PostgreSQL.
